WHAT IS
VIBES N VENTURES




In a nutshell it's a Reality Show about the lives of (3) Producers and what they produce. While in the pursuit of multiple Business Ventures the Trio find themselves constantly invited to VIP events, private functions to talk business, Health & Wellness gatherings or just a good old fashioned party. With a passion for telling #FeelGood stories and spotlighting worthy causes, "Vibes N Ventures" is here to invite you in to their Digital world to see first hand how Producers do it.


WHAT WE STAND FOR




In a world full of nonsense we stand to make sense. We look for the inspiring, the miraculous and the truth behind the untold. We value making new friends, strengthening current friendships and experiencing the adventures of everyday life in such a way as to bring enjoyment to those we encounter in a long lasting way. Truth in story telling is paramount to our identity as film makers.


WHO WE ARE




Creative Fun individuals would define all 3 of us. Collectively we are (3) Friends from very diverse backgrounds who seamlessly interact with each other to enhance the others positive attributes. Neil, Noah and Debbie have come together to form "3 Media Productions" a production company to produce their flagship show "Vibes N Ventures" as well as the Television shows of other Artists.
