California King Palm Four Seasons Canopy

The California King Palm Four Seasons frame and top is the strongest canopy we make. The California King Palm Four Seasons frame and top is manufactured with a reinforced, powder coated, aluminum commercial grade frame. It comes equipped with two mesh covered window cut-outs, providing ultimate flexibility. Constructed with flame retardent fabric, the four seasons is built to last. Unzip the windows to provide sunlight under the canopy or to allow wind to pass through. When the sun is too bright or if rain is in the forecast, the windows can be zipped shut providing a barrier between you and the elements. It has a weight of 92 lbs.

More and more states are adopting California's strict fire safety standards applicable to vendors using canopies/tents for public events such fairs, farmers markets, carnivals, parades and other similar events. In most counties throughout California, vendors are required to use flame retardant canopies. California Palms Inc. is proud to be one of the distributors of flame retardant canopies, and one of the select, few distributors that have the Fire Marshall's approval for Multi-Color Flame Retardant Canopies.

NOTE:

To meet the California Fire Marshall standard on being fire retardant the fabric is unlike normal Plastic and/or fabric used on our normal canopy covers. These canopy tops are not sprayed with a PVC coating to fill in the slight differences in the alignment of threading in the fabric. Therefore, on a sunny day, little spots of light may be visible but is not a cause of concern as it does not affect the functionality of the canopy.

Please be advised:

Our Flame Retardant Canopy Tops are made water resistant from light rain, and are not meant to be waterproof and be used in heavy rain.
