About Me

PAST EXPERIENCE

Graphic Design - Web Design - App Design
  • DESIGN

  • APP

My graphic design career began in high school. I took one class in digital imaging and immediately found joy in visual design.

I began by simple photography and image enchancing. I would be hired to take photos at special events and photshop images that clients selected.

My interest in web design began with my mySpace account. Being about to customize, redesign and reposition mySpace was very entertaining for me. I found coding to be much more interesting than digital imaging. Being able to create so much with just letters and numbers was fun.

Learning how to code is like learning a new langauge. I decided to take community college courses on web design which then lead me to transfer to Mt. Sierra College.

I learned how to develop applications in my interactice design courses at Mt. Sierra College. Shortly after I wanted to create my on app for musicians. One of my professors was impress with the fast progress I was making and recommended me to make a appliation for the school.

I was hired by Mt. Sierra College to make their app in Spring 2016. Currently I am still developing their application and updating content on the schools websites.
